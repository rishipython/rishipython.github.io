## ALLNet — Hybrid CNN for ALL Diagnosis *(Dec 2021)*
- Python, TensorFlow/Keras. 92% accuracy for Acute Lymphocytic Leukemia (ALL) from WBC images.
- Published in **IEEE BIBM 2021** *(4 citations)*.


## Parkinson’s Diagnosis via GB + mRMR *(Oct 2022)*
- Gradient Boosting with scikit‑learn on patient audio features; **90% accuracy**.
- Published in **Future Technologies Conference (FTC)**.


## One Eye is All You Need — Gaze Estimation *(Nov 2022)*
- SqueezeNet + PyTorch on AWS; single‑eye tracking.
- **1.4 cm** error (two eyes); **2.3 cm** (single eye). Comparable to MIT/Google results. *(12 citations)*
